Twitter
Sentiment Analysis

Feedbacks are the most important factors and are in the decision making process for organizations both big and small. Social media especially platforms like Twitter have become a major source for such feedback. Hence, analysis fo the reviews posted (or tweeted) on Twitter can help organizations understand their customers/consumers better and use this information to impprove the same. In this project, I used the concepts of Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to classify sample Tweets consisting of reviews about global Airline companies into positive, negative or neutral reviews. This model can potentially assist organizations to make more informed choiced based on the feedback due to recent changes.

Using Logistic Regression

In this situtation, Logistic Regression is the popular choice in cases where the nature of the dependent variable is dichotomous in nature. The analysis of the attributes can help identifying the characteristics required to survive shipwrecks.

Highschool Dropout Prediction
with machine learning

The advent of machine learning and the availability of an immense amount of data have enabled the development of data science and consequently, its application in educational institutions. Educational data mining enables the educator/teacher to monitor student requirement and provides the necessary response and counselling. In this paper, I used advance machine learning algorithms like logistic regression, decision trees and K-nearest neighbours to predict whether a student will drop out or continue his/her education.
